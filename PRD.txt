# PRD — Single-Shop Food Ordering Platform

## 1. Project Overview

Build a modern, mobile-first food ordering platform for a single food business selling:

* Snacks
* Fast Food
* Tiffins
* Beverages
* Special Items

The platform should provide an experience similar to Swiggy/Zomato for a single vendor while remaining simple to manage.

---

# 2. Goals

### Business Goals

* Accept online orders directly
* Accept online payments through Razorpay
* Manage menu without developer support
* Increase repeat customers
* Reduce phone-order dependency
* Track sales and customer activity

### User Goals

* Browse menu easily
* Place orders quickly
* Pay online securely
* Track order progress
* Reorder previous purchases
